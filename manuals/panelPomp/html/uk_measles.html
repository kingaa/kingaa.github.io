uk_measles {panelPomp}R Documentation

Weekly reported measles data for 362 locations in the UK

Description

Weekly reported measles data for 362 locations in the UK

Usage

uk_measles

Format

uk_measles

A list of 3 data frames Unit names ending in .RD are for rural areas; other unit names are for urban areas. NOTE: not all units have coordinates.

uk_measles$measles:

unit

City name

data

Date of observation

cases

Number of measles cases reported during the week

uk_measles$demog:

unit

City name

year

Year that demography was recorded

pop

Population

births

Births

uk_measles$coord:

unit

City name

long

Longitude of city

lat

Latitude of city
